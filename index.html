01 About

Hello! I am a PhD student in MIT LIDS, co-advised by Professors Marzyeh Ghassemi and Collin Stultz. Prior to my PhD, I received my B.S.E. from Princeton University in 2023, where my senior thesis was supervised by the incredible Professor Christiane Fellbaum.

My research focuses on reliable and controllable machine learning with the goal of building models that behave predictably across diverse conditions. In particular, I find value in working on high-stakes domains like healthcare. I also enjoy drawing on tools from optimization, probability, and statistics in my work.

Outside of research, I love to read and have recently gotten into birding.

You can reach me at abinitha@mit.edu — I'd love to hear from you.
